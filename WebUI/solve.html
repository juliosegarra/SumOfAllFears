The Sum of All Fears

Input four numbers and a number K, then click the button. There will then be output verifying whether any two numbers from the list of four add up to the number K.

Example:
given [10, 15, 3, 7] and K of 17, return true since 10 + 7 is 1

Pattern:
[1st] + [2nd or 3rd or 4th] or [2nd] + ... = K; True or False?
